REDKOH Diagnostics Center

This App is intended to help you understand and troubleshoot your Precipitator. Whether you are Paper Mill, Power Plant, or a small Industrial plant your Precipitator can undergo the same performance difficulties. Thru a series of questions or by simply entering your meter reading Precipitator Controls Diagnostics can help you identify what is inhibiting your collection or causing opacity excursions. Redkoh Industries has been manufacturing electrostatic precipitator control systems for the world market for over 25 years. We have manufactured analog, solid state, and microprocessor type controls for the air pollution control industry in the USA and worldwide This app can save you precious down time and possibly eliminate the need for a service call, saving your company money. The following troubleshooting instructions are based on controls that have been installed correctly and have operated properly for a period of time. This is an early version of Precipitator Controls Diagnostics, in coming months there will be updates that include trouble shooting Rapper and Vibrator controls, and routine preventive maintenance of your precipitator. If you can’t resolve your precipitator issue, please contact us.
