Redkoh Industries has been manufacturing electrostatic precipitator controls for over 25 years. We have manufactured analog, solid state, and microprocessor type controls for the air pollution control industry in the USA and worldwide. At our facility in central New Jersey we manufacture our own electronic controls from circuit board design, through assembly, to complete control panel fabrication and testing.

As a designer and manufacturer, Redkoh is uniquely qualified to provide the technical expertise necessary to design your entire controls system. We also provide training, troubleshooting and repairs from our manufacturing facility in New Jersey. Custom site training is also available.

Redkoh now has the capability of supplying electrostatic precipitator spare parts. From Support, Anti-sway, and Feed-through Insulators to Access Doors to Transformer-rectifiers to Voltage Dividers to SCRs to CLRs to Vibrators and more. Redkoh can supply the items you need to keep your system up and running.
