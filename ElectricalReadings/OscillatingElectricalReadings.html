Oscillating Electrical Readings

This condition will definitely affect the performance of the Precipitator since any field not operating correctly will have impact on the outlet emissions. Possible causes of the above events are as follows:

  • Failed wire(s) in a wire and weight system.
  • Failed anti-sway insulators.
  • Poorly set parameters within the AVC.

COURSE OF ACTION:

  • Limit the output to below the spark over level and watching to see if the current fluctuates on a cyclical basis – If it does and the field can be held stable, this is probably the best result you can hope for at this stage.
  • Allowing the field to spark and watching to see if the response is a burst of sparks on a cyclical basis – If no amount of adjustment to the AVC parametrs will produce a stable response, your options are limited. Try reducing the power to a level where the sparking is minimized by using one of the AVC limits
  • Perform an internal inspection with particular attention to the following:
    • Antisway insulators
    • DE connections at the lower part of the frames
    • All other insulators
    • Broken Discharge electrodes
